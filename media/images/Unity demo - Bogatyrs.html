Unity demo

Vitaly •08 Aug 2014

Keeping records about the industrial process was renewed after a long break.

My long absence in the blog was caused by the fact that in May, after visiting the conference «DevGamm» in Moscow, I finally decided to move to Unity (the demo was previously developed on Air basis), and remake of the game began in all directions. Since Unity was a completely new programming environment for me, a lot of time was spent on learning the basics, testing methods, and in general on the «reinvention of the wheel». Everything had to be redone for Unity: elements of the environment (sky, clouds, and sun), landscape and buildings. Many things were initiated and finally all of them started to come together. Now we can sum up the results…


So, the following has been done for three months of the game development on Unity:

  • environment with a dynamic change of the time of day, and the appearance and hiding of the sun, moon and stars;
  • mix of clouds and sun and variable wind strength;
  • landscape with trees swaying in the wind, depending on the wind strength;
  • dynamic water and waterfalls;
  • construction, sale and destruction of buildings in the city-base of the player.

As it was promised in the previous post, the next step was the construction of an ancient fortress. However, everything else has also been changed.

Now Unity-version can be found in «Demo» section.
